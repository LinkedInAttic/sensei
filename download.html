Maven

We build snapshots to the Nexus Sonatype Snapshot repository at an ongoing basis until we do a full release, at which point we will sync to Maven central.

repository: https://oss.sonatype.org/content/repositories/snapshots/com/senseidb/sensei/

details:

<artifactId>sensei</artifactId>
